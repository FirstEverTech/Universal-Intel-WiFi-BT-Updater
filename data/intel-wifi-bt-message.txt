[Cyan] SUPPORT DEVELOPMENT
[Cyan] ===================

 Universal Intel Wi-Fi & Bluetooth Updater provides automated hardware
 detection and Intel wireless driver deployment for Intel Wi-Fi and BT
 adapters across supported Windows systems.

 The wireless driver database is continuously updated for accurate device
 recognition and compatibility across modern and legacy Windows systems.

 If this tool saved you time, improved wireless connectivity, or helped
 keep older hardware updated, please consider supporting development.
